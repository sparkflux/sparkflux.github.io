What is SparkFlux all about?

We believe in the inherent power of AI-centric solutions incorporating Large Language Models (LLMs) and the cloud in supercharging our everyday lives through question answering.

From listing the top asked questions for the exam you have been studying for to crafting a concise summary of your verbose company handbook, enabling you to regurgitate large amounts of information in a short time.

We aim to deliver niche products based on your use case, made possible by our patented streamlined MLOps pipeline which can be fed with your own data on-demand to cater to questions in your desired domain.

Just as we aim to skyrocket users’ efficiency through our AI product, we persistently preach and implement tactics internally to efficiently scale our development efforts both vertically and horizontally.

Through our many years of hands-on, production-grade development experience in the space of data, AI, and education, we believe in regular yet quality iteration of our product based on feedback which is aligned with our vision.
